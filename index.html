We teach R

We are Switzerland-based data scientists who have jointly spent over 25 years doing data science with R for both research and industry. Our goal is to help you learn the skills needed to discover and communicate insights from data and join the data revolution.

Courses

We offer various courses for everyone interested in starting their journey into Data Science. For more information see Upcoming courses.

Consulting

Have a Data Science problem? We are here to help. Send us a message and we will get back to you right away.

Upcoming courses

For everyone!

Balancing short, interactive presentations and longer hands-on, step-by-step practicals our courses are designed to cater to your specific level and needs, may you be a beginner or advanced data analyst. There will be multiple instructors to ensure that no one gets lost. Feel to contact us, if you have questions, and explore our open materials.

  • R for Data Science

    990CHF @ Uni Basel

    Learn how to do data science using R from the ground-up. After the course, you will know how to load data from external files, clean, organise, and explore data, conduct basic statistical analyses and create striking visualisations.
    Aug 24/25 | Register Nov 9/10 | Preregister

  • Statistics with R

    990CHF @ Uni Basel

    Learn how to make robust, actionable data-driven decisions from data using Statistics and R. After the course, you will be able to run various statistical tests and models, understand their assumptions and interpret their results.
    Sep 7/8 | Register

  • Machine Learning with R

    990CHF @ Uni Basel

    learn how to build predictive models using machine learning and R. After the course, you will understand the basics of machine learning and know how to apply various algorithms tot solve typical regression and classification problems.
